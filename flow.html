Grants for NGOs

Funding for independent nonviolence initiatives — for the work itself, or a tuition grant to study at Sibility Source.

What we fund

We back projects that reduce violence in families, schools and communities. We commit for the long term rather than a single cycle, so teams can plan real work — and grantees keep their freedom, including the freedom to choose other grantors.

Funding can go to the work itself — programmes, salaries, core running costs — or to a tuition grant, so someone on your team can study at Sibility Source and bring the practice back home. We try to fund what actually moves your mission forward, not what happens to fit a rigid template.

We know the smallest organisations often carry the most weight with the least support. There is no minimum track record and no requirement to be a registered charity — what matters is that your work genuinely reduces violence, and that you can describe how it does.

Applications are open on a rolling basis. Clear conditions, a clear process, and decisions explained — every answer, yes or no, comes with reasons. Professionals talking with professionals.
