What we teach

Nonviolence is a capacity — something you can learn, practise and get better at. Our courses run from short introductions to in-depth programmes, taught by practitioners and researchers who work in the field every day.

Some are strange on purpose: they start from real situations rather than theory, and ask you to try things, not just take notes. You leave with something you can actually use the same week — at home, in your team, or in the community.

Courses come in two tracks — one built for organisations working in the field, and one for anyone who simply wants to handle conflict better in their own life. A number of places are covered by Sibility Flow tuition grants.
