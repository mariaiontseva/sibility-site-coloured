The word
sibility
/sɪˈbɪl.ɪ.ti/ · noun

The capacity of a person — or a community — to live, act and resolve conflict without violence.

Not yet in the dictionary — that is why we are here.
How we work

Three commitments hold everything we do.

Resilience

We build for the long term — steady support that initiatives can plan around.

Flexibility

We never require anyone to do what we consider the only right way — grantees keep their freedom, including the freedom to choose other grantors.

Professionalism

Clear conditions, clear process, decisions explained. We work as professionals with professionals.
