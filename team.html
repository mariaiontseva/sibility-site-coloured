About us

We are a small organisation with one stubborn belief: that nonviolence is a skill the world can be taught. We fund the people already doing that work, teach it openly, study what actually helps, and make it easy for anyone to take one real step.

Why we exist

Most harm doesn’t start as violence — it starts small, and by the time it has a name it is already hard to undo. Sibility works upstream of that moment.

Four ways in, one purpose: Flow funds the field, Source teaches the skill, Delta studies the evidence, and Current turns it into something you can do this week.
